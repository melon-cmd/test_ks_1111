## 背景故事

### Meet NocFree &

### Feel Natural Comfort

Which typing posture feels more comfortable?

We all have different body types and habits. But traditional keyboards ask us to fit in. NocFree & lets you open your hands to shoulder width and rotate each half to match your natural posture. You’ll see your hands, wrists, and forearms align into a relaxed, continuous line.

Our tenting kit add-on lifts each half at an angle that restores the forearm’s natural rotation—like how your hand rests on an ergonomic mouse.

With NocFree &, a simple wrist adjustment can reshape how you sit, type, and feel —all day long.

You’ll feel less pressure in your chest and shoulders, and reduce the tendency to hunch forward.

You’ve already invested in an ergonomic chair, and that’s a great start. Now let your keyboard complete the comfort.

### Smooth Gasket Feel

The feel and sound of every keystroke shapes how you experience your work.

That's why we created NocFree & to bring you both ergonomic comfort and a typing experience you'll love every day.

These cushions every keystroke, reducing vibration and creating a smoother, quieter, more refined typing experience.

It’s a structure typically found in premium mechanical keyboards—rarely, if ever, in compact low-profile split ones. That’s what makes NocFree & different.

To complete the feel, we partnered with a switch manufacturer to develop our own NocFree low-profile switches. They’re light, smooth, and tuned to match the overall ergonomic experience.

Our 40 gf low-profile linear switches are easier on the fingers thanks to three key factors: a lighter actuation force, shorter travel compared to standard mechanical switches, and a smooth linear spring curve that reduces sudden pressure changes.

Together, these features provide a more comfortable and less fatiguing typing experience than both full-height mechanical switches and typical laptop keyboards.

For those who prefer a quieter typing experience, we’ve unlocked the NocFree Silent Switch together!

Just as smooth, but lighter and far quieter .

This option has been unlocked since our campaign reached $100K 👏 See our Stretch Goals for details.

### Flexible Placement

The space between the halves is more than empty desk. It’s where your tools, thoughts, and lifestyle take shape. What do you want to put there?

Need number entry, custom macros, or quick shortcuts?

Just place the standalone numpad wherever you want—and make it work your way.

Traditional keyboards with built-in numpads force your mouse too far to the right—causing strain in your shoulder, arm, and neck. With NocFree &, you keep your setup balanced and your posture natural.

### Crafted to Impress

This time, we partnered with one of the top CNC aluminum case manufacturers in the custom keyboard world to deliver a premium finish.

Why #150-grit anodizing instead of #180 or even #205?

After extensive testing, we found that true #205‑grit sandblasting isn’t practical for keyboard cases. At such fine grit, even a light touch from a fingernail can leave scratches — making it unsuitable for mass production or daily use.

While some brands claim a #205 finish, we prefer to be transparent: ours is #150. A well‑executed #150‑grit anodized finish offers the ideal balance of texture, durability, and feel.

We also added a distinctive visual detail:

A mirror-polished concave badge . First, the entire curved surface is polished to a high gloss. Then, everything except the & is laser-etched to create a subtle, layered highlight.

And here’s something you’ve probably never seen:

NocFree & can stand upright on your desk, like a piece of functional art.

Our tenting kit isn’t just ergonomic. It also doubles as a stand, letting you display your keyboard when not in use—and now it even adds charging to the mix.

Made from the same premium aluminum and designed, it’s clean, clever, and just feels right.

Type in comfort, charge with ease, and display in style.

To match different desk setups, 7 color options will be available for backers to choose from.

We’ve just hit $200,000, which means our third stretch goal is officially unlocked. 🎉

And the best part? The choice is yours.

Inside the box, we’ve included 3 colorful accent caps.

A small splash of color is all it takes to make the minimalist design stand out.

### Instant Productivity

NocFree Link is our intuitive configuration software, designed for today’s keyboards. It makes every key on your NocFree split keyboard fully programmable.

Beginners can set up keymaps and shortcuts in minutes with the web‑based configurator. Advanced users can explore the desktop client for app‑based profiles, global shortcuts, macros, and up to eight custom layers.

It works on macOS, Windows, and Linux , giving you a simple and powerful way to customize your NocFree.

Your settings are stored directly on the keyboard , so they stay with you no matter which device you use.

Worried about the steep learning curve of split keyboards?

NocFree & makes the transition smooth, so you can feel the ergonomic benefits from day one.

NocFree & uses a familiar 84-key 75% layout —complete with dedicated F1–F12 keys. There’s no need to relearn where things are. Everything’s right where you expect it.

### ISO Layout

ISO layout in a low-profile split keyboard? That’s almost unheard of.

NocFree & offers both ANSI and ISO versions—including dedicated layouts for UK-ISO, DE-ISO, Nordic-ISO, FR-ISO, IT-ISO, PT-ISO and ES-ISO users.

If ISO feels more natural to you, this might be the perfect entry point into split keyboards.

### Connect without Cable

Most split keyboards rely on open-source protocols—but they weren’t made for this.

We felt the gaps, so we built our own.

A proprietary wireless system, built from the ground up, to deliver a faster, smoother, and more reliable connection.

Think back to the early days of wireless earbuds. They were still tethered by a wire—until true wireless technology changed everything.

We believe split keyboards deserve the same evolution. And now, with your support, the true wireless 3-piece design is finally here with NocFree &.

Each year, countless new keyboards launch, and many with flashy names.

What is a keyboard?

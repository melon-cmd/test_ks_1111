### Long-lasting design

Anker SOLIX F3800 provides a reliable, clean, quiet, and affordable power source. The power station can replace noisy and polluting gas generators. It also promotes energy independence with sky-high electricity prices.
During blackouts, consumers can power their homes for days with the power station's massive battery capacity. It also provides flexible recharging options. Consumers can use an AC outlet or solar power. With InfiniPower™ technology, Anker SOLIX F3800 is built to last up to 10 years, providing quality and confidence to consumers.

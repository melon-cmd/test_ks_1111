### Product Highlights

Dnsys Z1 features a top-level assistive system that seamlessly combines high-performance hardware and intelligent software. With integrated torque, position, and force sensors, its assistance activates within 0.01 seconds , adapting to your movements with unparalleled precision , delivering smooth , seamless , and precise support across all kinds of outdoor terrain.

Z1 recognizes and switches modes for walking, running, jumping, squatting, sitting, or standing . It intelligently adapts to flat ground, slopes, stairs, trails, forests, or gravel for natural support. The longer you use Z1, the more it learns your gait over time—whether your steps are light and subtle or large and uneven — for personalized stability. Backed by thousands of hours of testing , it detects abnormalities to prevent falls and keeps you safe.

Dnsys’ Smart AI Motion Assist uses DNNAS (Dynamic Neural Network Assist System) deep learning algorithm. Trained on real-world data from over 400 professional testers and more than 1,500 hours of motion trials, the system employs thousands of neurons to anticipate your movements—refining its support through insights gathered from over 5,000 active users . This ensures personalized gait assistance, smoothly adapting to challenging terrains for stable, natural support.

The AI system runs on a dual-core 240 MHz processor and works in tandem with two high-speed motor control processors. Together, they predict muscle activation in real-time and drive precise torque output using a 40,000 Hz FOC algorithm. This ensures real-time response and precise output, allowing you to experience stable, seamless, and efficient assistance across all movements and terrains.

Driven by the pursuit of ultimate performance, we engineered the DNA-1 motor — Z1’s core . Crafted and patented for high-end wearable robots, it integrates seamlessly. Designed to be ultra-lightweight, precise, and powerful, it surpasses MIT's Mini Cheetah* motor in performance, offering greater power , lighter weight , and higher efficiency .

* Mini Cheetah's self-developed motors are known for being compact yet powerful.

With 1.2 horsepower and 50 Nm/kg of torque , achieving a power-to-weight ratio of 0.76—on par with that of a Formula One race car—it delivers the most powerful motor output among wearable exoskeletons. Even users with heavier body weights will feel strong and ample power, but if you don’t require maximum strength, there’s no need to worry—Z1’s power output is fully adjustable to suit your needs.

The DNA-1 motor provides compliance via AI for interpreting your movements, offering precise and comfortable assistance. Its patented design and gear materials reduce friction, minimizing coil current transmission loss. The high-efficiency patented gearbox boosts force control precision by 32% and lowers resistance by 26% . With a high reduction ratio, the motor runs under loads without significant heat, extending battery life and reducing failure rates. Paired with a high-bandwidth magnetic field-orientation control system, it ensures comfort and safety at all times.

Dnsys Z1 blends firm exoskeleton support with soft, flexible adaptability. Carbon fiber at key support and connection points ensures durability and reliable performance in harsh conditions. Flexible 3D-knitted fabric combined with a ribbed structure fits comfortably and securely on any terrain. This innovative design balances firmness and softness, syncing seamlessly with your body movements.

Z1's exclusive three-point support structure offers a highly stable wearing experience with an ultra-streamlined design. It efficiently transfers motor power to stronger, more stable parts of the body, delivering powerful assistance while reducing overall weight. The result is enhanced performance with virtually no interference to daily movement and no added burden on any part of the body.

Thanks to its unique design and lightweight materials, the Z1 weighs just 1.5 pounds , making it the lightest exoskeleton on the market without sacrificing durability. It fits easily into a backpack and can be put on in just 15 seconds . Its featherlight build ensures all-day comfort without bulk or pressure. It gives you a close, stay-in-place fit that holds all day—secure and stable even during intense movement, no matter how hard you jump.

With its modular design, the Z1 can be adjusted to fit your body shape for a more customized and secure fit. The built-in algorithm automatically calibrates based on size changes, ensuring a consistent support experience regardless of body type or wearing style.

The Dnsys Z1 is equipped with a groundbreaking knee support system that helps reduce stress on the lower limb joints. By easing the physical load, it can also help lower your heart rate and support overall cardiovascular health.

The Dnsys app enables you to easily manage the Z1, monitor the device status in real time, and track medical-grade health data such as knee joint mobility and step frequency. This allows you to customize your training plans, review your past progress, and ultimately achieve peak performance on every adventure.

Dnsys Z1’s high-density lithium-ion batteries with KERS (Kinetic Energy Recovery System) enable downhill regenerative charging— offering an extra 9.3 miles of range on mountain terrain. ### Coordinated X1 and Z1

In 2024, we launched the Dnsys X1 hip exoskeleton, designed for speed and endurance , significantly improving efficiency with incremental leg lift, helping you walk farther, faster, and with less effort.

Following the X1, many users praised its performance and requested a knee-specific exoskeleton that eases pressure, improves stability, and provides standing support.

That's why we created the Dnsys Z1 knee exoskeleton — built for power and protection . It boosts your steps, relieves knee pressure, and makes you feel 44 lbs lighter. It also improves gait stability and posture, making it ideal for people with weak knees, joint issues, or anyone needing extra lower-limb support.

X1 and Z1 focus on different needs, catering to various user requirements. If you want to boost endurance while gaining stronger lower-limb assistance, the X1 and Z1 can be worn together . Thanks to Dnsys’s exclusive smart algorithm (not compatible with other brands) , the two devices coordinate their power output to enhance overall support, making it the world’s first consumer-grade exoskeleton with coordinated hip-and-knee assistance . However, we recommend switching between the Z1 and X1 based on different usage scenarios to achieve the best experience.

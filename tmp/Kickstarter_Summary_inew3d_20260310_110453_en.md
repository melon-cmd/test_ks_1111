Developing and delivering advanced full-color 3D printing technology involves several challenges, including manufacturing scale-up, material optimization, and software refinement.

To address these risks, we have completed multiple rounds of internal testing on QC2A’s hardware, color system, and material workflow, and continue to validate reliability through long-term stress tests. We are working with experienced manufacturing partners and have built contingency time into our production schedule.

Material profiles and software features may continue to be refined during the campaign, but all changes are aimed at improving stability, print quality, and user experience.

As with any hardware project, unexpected delays may occur, but we are committed to transparent communication and regular updates throughout the process.
